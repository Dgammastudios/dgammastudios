D'gamma Studios

Welcome to my studio! I've been building skills and my equipment collection for years. When I was about 14 years old, I mounted a speaker and battery powered amplifier into a backpack and started making beats on iMaschine on an iPod. Now, I run a professionally equipped and fully capable studio.

I mix primarily in the box with plugins from some of the best brands, including Waves, Brainworx, Melodyne, and Black Rooster Audio. I have plugins of sounds that have made their way to countless platinum records, including Neve's 1073 and 1084 channel strips, SSL's 9000 J console, the gold standard 1176 and LA-2A compressors, and much more. My hardware highlights a dozen mics, including a modded large diaphram tube mic (check it out below), a Beyerdynamic M 201 TG (a detailed a clear dynamic mic for vocals, voiceovers, and instruments), a tube channel strip for that coveted warm sound, and more. I also have several virtual instruments, including Synthmaster 2, studio quality sampled pianos, orchestral instruments, drum kits, UJam's Carbon (virtual guitarist), UJam's Rowdy (virtual bassist), and more!

Book your sessions today! The wood panel walls gives my studio energetic and clean recordings, and my monitors and subwoofer are calibrated and opimally placed for the best mixing environment. Welcoming and comfortable, I've done my best to turn D'gamma Studios into a space for creative expression and for an immersive experience.

As a temporary opening special, hourly rates are $45/hr, 25% off! There are also a limited amount of discounts for project rates each month too, with as much as 58% off project rates for the first booking of the month. Contact me at d.gammastudios@gmail.com to book today!

Hardware


Modded Large Diaphram Condenser Tube Mic
In my studio, this wonderful mic is the centerpiece. The mods improved the warmth of the mic, improved it's frequency response, and reduced noise. It hosts the same 6072 type vacuum tube found in mics like the Telefunken Ela M 251, and it has a 32mm capsule with the same style as the Neve U67. The result is a warm, detailed mic, with an incredibly smooth frequency response, a beautiful richness, and a hint of air.

First Ridgeland Christmas 2021

I had the honor of mixing the Christmas songs for First Ridgeland Baptist Church in 2021. We recorded everything like it was live, with about 3 takes for each song. We recorded half the songs on Monday, the other half on Wednesday, and I finished mixing by that Friday night so we could send it in to run on local TV! This project was rushed and recorded live, and it marks one of my steps transitioning from working as a live sound engineer into the world of the recording studio.

While I'm not currently advertising myself as a producer, I do have experience in songwriting and sound design. I've played drums since I was probably 9 years old, and I love translating musicality and expression into sample based drum kits. I can also play piano, know a good amount of music theory, and design my own synths, synth basses, and 808 sounds.
