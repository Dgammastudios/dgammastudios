Photography

I was first introduced to photography in high school when I got a phone with a 23 megapixel camera and a few professional settings, like shutter speed, ISO, and white balance. Later, I started learning more from a friend who freelanced and worked summers as a photographer for Lake Forest Ranch. I got pretty comfortable shooting with a Sony a6400, and was able to learn about framing, f-stop, focal depth, and depth of field. Later, I started doing photo shoots for my girlfriend, who I eventually convinced to be my wife. She does photography for the Mississippi College Art Department, and for weddings. I've helped with a few photo shoots at Mississippi College and have run as her second when shooting weddings.
