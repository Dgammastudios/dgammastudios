Responding to COVID-19


April 18, 2020 update

To do my part in keeping you safe and healthy, I am currently not open for working weddings. I will post an update as things return to normal. Until then, booking to DJ wedding receptions and booking for wedding ceremony sound are still open. As we wait for the effects of this epidemic to settle, I understand that we need to prepare to get back to normal, and I understand plans may need to change to continue putting health and safety first. So, if you book now and end out changing your wedding date, you can still book with D'gamma Studios at no risk. I won't be asking for booking deposits until further notice incase your plans need to change. So, continue planning for your big day with D'gamma Studios, at no risk. For inquiries, email me at D.gammastudios@gmail.com, or call me at (662) 722-0205. I hope you all stay healthy! We're in this together!
