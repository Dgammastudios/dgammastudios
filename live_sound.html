Live Sound




I've worked in various positions in live sound since 2018. I started DJing weddings, and I worked as the audio engineer for my church at the time, where a Jackson, MS music studio owner showed me the ropes. After graduating from college in 2020, I started hosting small shows so local artists could show off thier original music. I also got a job as the lead audio engineer at First Ridgeland, in Ridgeland, MS. I continued working live sound there, running contemporary services from the spring of 2020 until the spring of 2023.

I still ocassionally work as a live sound engineer, and I still occasionally DJ. I own all of my own sound equipment, and have enough power to run an event comfortably loud for 200-400 people, depending on a few other factors. I am now based out of Pontotoc, MS, and plan on continuing to invest in the local music scene.
