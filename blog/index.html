Welcome to my Blog!

I’m starting a blog on my journey as I graduate from college. I am now in a big life transition as I pursue a career to make a positive impact on the world around me. In the mean time, I'm finding out how to pay taxes, find work, pay bills, and learn valuable skills. As I continue blogging, I'll be asking, "how do we grow our value community and how do we grow the value of selflessness in our busy world full of distractions." This is when I say, I really have no idea what I'm doing. I'm a young adult with a website and a blog and a couple ideas to make a valuable investment with my time. Enjoy!
